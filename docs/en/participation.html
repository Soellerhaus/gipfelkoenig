Participation Terms

Last updated: March 2026

1. Eligibility

  • Participation is open to natural persons aged 16 or older.
  • Persons under 18 require the consent of a legal guardian.
  • Participation is free and requires registration at bergkoenig.app.
  • Only one account per person is allowed. Multiple accounts will result in exclusion.
  • Employees of Bergkönig and their relatives are excluded from raffles.

2. Game Mechanics & Points

  • Bergkönig is a peak-collecting game for the Alpine region.
  • Points are earned by ascending peaks.
  • Point calculation is based on: elevation gain (EG/100), distance (km × 1), peak bonus (+10), and multipliers (Pioneer ×3, First visit ×2, Repeat ×0.2).
  • Additional bonuses: Early bird (tour before 07:00, +15 pts), Combo (2+ peaks/day), Streak (weekly activity).
  • GPS data is used exclusively for summit detection and is not stored.
  • Manipulation (faked GPS data, bots, multiple accounts) leads to immediate exclusion.

3. Raffle System

  • Each ascended peak = 1 raffle ticket.
  • Pioneer ascent (first of the season) = 3 raffle tickets.
  • King of a peak = 5 raffle tickets per crown.
  • Active weekly streak = 1 raffle ticket per week.
  • Peak of the day ascended = 3 raffle tickets.
  • Raffles will only take place once 500 participants are registered.
  • Raffles are held at the end of the season (December 31) via random number generator.
  • There is no legal entitlement to the conduct of raffles.
  • Cash payout of prizes is excluded.

4. Prizes & Sponsors

  • All prizes are provided by sponsors and partners.
  • Bergkönig assumes no warranty or liability for the prizes.
  • Complaints about prizes should be directed to the respective sponsor/manufacturer.
  • The type and scope of prizes may vary and will be announced before the raffle.

5. Winner Notification

  • Winners will be notified by email to the address provided during registration.
  • Winners have 14 days to accept the prize. After the deadline, a replacement winner will be drawn.
  • The winner's username will only be published in the app with explicit consent.

6. Data Protection

  • For participation, only email address and username are stored.
  • GPS tracks are processed for summit detection but not stored. Only derived results (peak, timestamp, elevation gain) are stored.
  • Personal data is not shared with sponsors or third parties.
  • In case of a win, the email address is used exclusively for contact and prize delivery.
  • Further details: Privacy Policy

7. Liability Disclaimer

Bergkönig is a game — not a mountain guide and not a safety tool.
  • Participation in Bergkönig is at your own risk.
  • Bergkönig assumes no liability for accidents, injuries, property damage, or other damages arising in connection with mountain sports.
  • Participants are personally responsible for their safety in the mountains.
  • The avalanche situation must be checked independently at lawinen.report or with local authorities.
  • Bergkönig does not replace professional route planning, mountain guides, or Alpine clubs.
  • The safety system in the app (avalanche danger level) serves only as an indication and is not binding.
  • Bergkönig is not liable for the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of data displayed in the app (peak positions, elevation data, routes, safety information).

8. Exclusion & Violations

  • Bergkönig reserves the right to exclude participants for violations of these terms at any time and without giving reasons.
  • The following actions in particular lead to exclusion: manipulation of GPS data, use of bots, creation of multiple accounts, insulting other participants.
  • Upon exclusion, all collected points, raffle tickets, and prize claims are forfeited.

9. Changes

  • Bergkönig reserves the right to change these Participation Terms, game rules, and the points system at any time.
  • Significant changes will be announced in the app.
  • Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.

10. Final Provisions

  • Austrian law applies.
  • Place of jurisdiction is Feldkirch, Austria.
  • Legal recourse is excluded.
  • Should individual provisions be invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain unaffected.
